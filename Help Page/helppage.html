About GLOBAL TIMES e-Paper

GLOBAL TIMES e-Paper is a digital solution brought to you by GLOBAL Vertos-Shining buds, enabling publishers to reach their readership through innovative digital and networking solutions.

GLOBAL TIMES e-Paper is the most convenient, complete and cost-effective way to read all your favourite sections of newspaper on one site. With a subscription of GLOBAL TIMES e-Paper, users gain access to newspaper on the day they are published. It enhance your reading experience with a simple user interface and easy-to-use navigation tools.

GLOBAL TIMES e-Paper provides a global perspective on the world's events, by delivering a wide range of news to readers, while at home and away.

For accessing the content...

  1. Make your account by clicking on Sign Up.
  2. After filling it, login and you are eligible to see the news afterwards.
  3. In case you forget the password, click on forget password right below Login button and give the required details (email id) and link will be send. Further fill in your new password and confirm it. Now, you are again eligible to continue the access to news.
